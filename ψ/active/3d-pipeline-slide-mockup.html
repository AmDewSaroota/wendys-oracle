6 HANDOFF

Your Model Isn't For You 🤝

The handoff checklist — what we do before passing the model:

  • ✅ Clean mesh — no stray verts, no overlapping faces
  • ✅ Correct pivot points — door hinge at the hinge, not center
  • ✅ Face direction — all normals facing outward
  • ✅ Proper naming — Project_Asset_v03_date
  • ✅ Talk to the next person — "How do you want this?"

7 NAMING

Name It Like a Pro 📁

❌ What we've all done:

😱 Bad Naming Example model_final.fbx
model_final_v2.fbx
model_FINAL_REAL.fbx
model_FINAL_REAL_fixed.fbx

✅ What you should do:

✨ Good Naming Example JobVR_EspressoMachine_v01_20260401.fbx
JobVR_EspressoMachine_v02_20260405.fbx
JobVR_EspressoMachine_v03_20260410.fbx
💡 Tip: Your file name should tell someone WHAT it is, WHICH version, and WHEN — without opening it.
8 COMMUNICATION

Communication is Your Superpower 💬

As a Leader 👑

  • 🔓 Crack open vague requirements — "make it cool" → actual brief
  • 📋 Break it down for the team — clear tasks, clear deadlines
  • 🛡️ Shield team from client chaos — you handle the changes

As a Team Member 🤝

  • 🚨 Speak up when there's a problem — don't hide until deadline
  • 💡 Propose solutions, not just complaints
  • 🆘 Ask for help early — silence isn't strength, it's a bottleneck
"Don't be the person who makes problems. Be the person who makes everyone's job easier."

16 LESSONS

Mistakes We've All Made 😅

  • ❌ Detailing too early — blockout first!

  • ❌ Hiding problems instead of speaking up
  • ❌ Naming files "final_v2_REAL_thisone.fbx"
  • ❌ Forgetting scale — giant coffee cup in VR
