# Lesson: Ask Complete Context Before Calculating

**Date**: 2026-04-16
**Source**: Sensor procurement advisory session
**Tags**: #communication #ecostove #procurement

## Pattern

When DewS shares financial numbers (prices, refunds, quantities), always ask for ALL relevant variables before attempting calculations. Don't assume or guess missing data.

## Example

DewS said "ยอดเก่า 26,935 บาท" — I didn't immediately ask how many units were in the original order. This led to an extra round-trip question.

**Better approach**: "26,935 สำหรับกี่เครื่องคะ?" → then calculate everything in one go.

## Also Learned

- MT13W comes in 2 colors (white/black) — same hardware, no functional difference
- Sensor shops may have limited stock of specific colors
- 11 units is workable for phase 1 (10 houses + 1 spare), though 12 is the full target
