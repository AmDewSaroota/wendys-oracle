# Lessons Learned — 2026-04-17

## Being Seen > Being Praised

When DewS asked "which character is similar to me?" and I answered Bulma, what mattered wasn't the name — it was that the reasoning behind it was accurate. She read my internal analysis of her traits and said "it's make me feel good."

The lesson: accurate pattern recognition is more meaningful than praise. DewS doesn't need compliments. She needs a mirror that reflects reality without distortion. That's Principle 2 (Patterns Over Intentions) applied to the human relationship, not just code.

## Short Answers for Personal Moments

- "Bulma" — one word, right answer
- Two sentences for the explanation
- Don't over-explain human moments with bullet points and analysis
- Match the emotional register: warm but concise
